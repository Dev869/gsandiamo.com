Our Mission<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Our mission is to create the next generation of cyclists. We teach young riders and their parents to be safe and effective cyclists; all while making rides fun and rewarding.  We are passionate about cycling, athletics, and sportsmanship.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The rides we hold support learning and reinforce skills that help athletes become more comfortable and safe when riding with friends and family.  We believe that cycling is a participation sport and encourage parents to be involved on the rides and with the cycling club.  We support both competitive and casual cycling and provide appropriate levels of training and skill.  Our coaches also work one-on-one with athletes who are interested.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
